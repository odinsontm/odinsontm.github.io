The Leonardo Member Lounge

This project was apart of CLASS NUM HERE where a team of developers and designers worked together to create a website for a real life client. Our task was to create a membership portal for The Leonardo Museum. This website was in response to the need of a better membership solution for guests of the museum. The portal consisted of a dashboard, access to a digital membership card, newsletter, calendar of events, and exclusive content for members.

Completing this class gave me valuable experience in software design and devleopment. Additionally, this class gave me experience with working in a team of not only developers, but those who may not understand the technical parts of developing a website. Along with experience in creative problem solving skills when things did not go to plan, and how to manage and resolve group issues.


Number Ninja

This 2D platfromer was created as a part of CLASS NUM HERE. The goal of the class was to simulate a real world client building job. Our assignment was to create a game that helped 2nd and 3rd graders with their math skills. So, we came up with a 2D platformer where the user acted as a ninja running away from a dragon. Along the way, there were jumps where the user is propted with a math question, in order to land the jump the user would have to answer the question correctly.

This project gave my team members and I experience in software design and development as it applies to game development. It also gave me experience with working in a team, coordinating our work, and solving issues as they arose.
